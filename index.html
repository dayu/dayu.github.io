Iron Password

Password is generated as the first 12 characters of base64 encoding of SHA512 hash.

Suggest to use one secret phrase and website name to generate a unique password for each site. If additional special characters are required for the password, just add one like "~" as prefix and postfix.

Simple public algorithm to generate password. You can always recover your password as long as you remember your secret phrase even if this site is down.

With SHA512 hashing, even if your password for one website is leaked, it will take years for supercomputers to calculate your secrete phrase and steal identity.
